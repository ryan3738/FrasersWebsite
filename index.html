About

FRASERS GOURMET HIDEAWAY
is owned and operated by Chef Scott Fraser. Guests will notice a relaxing environment where food and drinks are the focal point. Come enjoy a seasonal menu of the freshest local Northwest ingredients. Pair this with a fabulous, vibrant West Coast wine or a nice cold beer.
